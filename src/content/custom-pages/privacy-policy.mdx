This policy describes how **Monto IA** collects, uses, and protects the personal data of visitors to monto.ia.br, in compliance with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (LGPD — Law No. 13,709/2018).

## 2. Data We Collect

### Contact Form

When you submit the contact form, we collect your name, email address, subject, and message. This information is securely transmitted to our email delivery service (Resend) and is not stored in our own database.

### Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to analyze site traffic in aggregate. Data collected includes: pages visited, session duration, approximate location, and device type. No personally identifiable data is collected by Google Analytics without your consent.

### Security Verification — Cloudflare Turnstile

Our contact form uses **Cloudflare Turnstile**, an anti-spam verification service. Turnstile analyzes browser signals to confirm you are human, without displaying disruptive visual CAPTCHAs. By using the form, you agree to the [Cloudflare Turnstile Privacy Policy Addendum](https://www.cloudflare.com/en-gb/turnstile-privacy-policy/).

## 3. Purpose of Processing

| Data | Purpose | Legal Basis (LGPD) |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Contact form | Respond to your message | Legitimate interest / Contract performance |
| Analytics | Improve the site and understand usage | Legitimate interest |
| Cloudflare Turnstile | Prevent spam and abuse | Legitimate interest |

## 4. Data Sharing

Your data may be processed by the following service partners, each subject to their own privacy policy:

- **Resend** — transactional email delivery ([privacy policy](https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy))
- **Google** — analytics and aggregate tracking ([privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Cloudflare** — security, hosting, and Turnstile ([privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/))

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

Contact form data is kept only for as long as necessary to respond to your request. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics policies (default: 14 months).

## 6. Your Rights (LGPD — Art. 18)

As a data subject, you have the right to:

- **Confirmation** — know whether your data is being processed
- **Access** — obtain a copy of your data
- **Correction** — fix incomplete or incorrect data
- **Deletion** — request removal of unnecessary data
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured format
- **Withdrawal** — revoke consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: **contato@monto.ia.br**

## 7. Security

We adopt technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or improper disclosure. All communication between your browser and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The date of the last update will always be indicated at the top of this document. We recommend checking this page periodically.
